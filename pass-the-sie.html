📚 What is the SIE Exam?

The Securities Industry Essentials (SIE) exam is a foundational exam for anyone looking to enter the securities industry. It's designed to test your knowledge of basic securities industry concepts.

Key Facts:

  • Format: 75 multiple-choice questions
  • Time: 105 minutes
  • Passing Score: 70%
  • Prerequisite: None - anyone can take it
  • Validity: 4 years

Topics Covered:

  • Securities Products & Markets
  • Investment Risks
  • Regulation & Compliance
  • Client Accounts & Suitability
  • Professional Conduct & Ethics

📖 SIE Exam Resources

Official Links:

  • FINRA.org - Official regulator
  • SEC.gov - Securities & Exchange Commission

Study Tips:

  • Focus on suitability rules and regulations
  • Understand options strategies thoroughly
  • Know the different account types (IRAs, 401k, etc.)
  • Practice with as many questions as possible
